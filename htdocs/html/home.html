Paul Carrick

Paul lives in Eastsound, Washington, on beautiful Orcas Island. He is a husband, father, and grandfather who brings warmth and insight into every role he plays. Professionally, Paul is a Senior Software Engineer with a rich career background. Originally from Southern California, he attended College of the Canyons, Biola University, and California State University, Northridge.

In 1997, Paul joined Amazon.com when it was a budding startup. He played a key role in developing Amazon’s foundational shipping systems, helping to shape the customer experience. Outside of work, Paul has a passion for travel, cooking, music, and photography, which continues to inspire his life and work.
